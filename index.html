Approach

We understand that every opportunity is different and we tailor our approach to address your specific needs by working closely with you. We also understand that results matter. The keys to successful engagements are establishing great relationships and mutual understanding of the objectives and measures for success.

Our engagements start with a discussion on the problem we are trying to solve / your project. We will work closely with you to understand the current state and environment as well as where you need to be at the conclusion of the engagement.

When we achieve a shared understanding, we will collaboratively establish and gain conceptual agreement on the objectives, measures, outcomes, and value to the organization.

Finally, we will submit for review and approval, a unique proposal specifically designed to address your specific outcomes.

Commitments

We commit to only engaging in work where we can add significant value and contribute to your profitability.

We commit to providing regular updates and measures to gauge progress during the engagement and ensure we hit our agreed upon milestones.

We seek to educate your team whenever possible, to enable the transfer of skills over time, and to reduce dependence on Radioactive.io over time so that there is lasting value after we are gone.

We recognize the need for your confidentiality with regard to proprietary technology, methodologies, and information and we commit to protect it.

We believe that shared responsibility is the key to a successful relationship with you.

About

Radioactive.io is operated by Ken Haigh, the President and Founder. Ken has a strong track record of success for over 22 years of experience in software and SaaS specializing in process improvement, product lifecycle, product management, design, technology architecture and vision, innovation management, and quality assurance in venture backed technology companies. Previously, Ken served as the COO and CTO of PeopleMatter, a fast-growing SaaS Company in the HCM Space. Prior to PeopleMatter, Ken led an 85-person staff with a $7.5m budget responsible for the design, engineering, and quality assurance activities of Blackbaud Enterprise CRM during his tenure at Blackbaud, Inc.. He is well-versed in mobile technology with over 15 years experience in the mobile space leading teams while at Intellisync Corp (formerly Synchrologic, Inc.). Ken started his career as a Software Engineer at Lotus Development Corporation working on Ami Pro, an early pioneer and the first full featured graphical word processor.
